Game Manual & Specifications

Welcome to the ultimate retro HTML5 2D canvas football experience. Engineered with custom physics vectors, dynamic scale calculations, and progressive algorithm difficulty, this game brings intense penalty action straight to your browser container.

Game Modes & Mechanics

  • 1 Player Arcade Mode: Test your individual execution against an adaptively accelerating AI goalkeeper. Score as many consecutive penalty kicks as possible to build a streak multiplier. Missing a single shot wraps up your session instantly.
  • 2 Player Local Mode: Grab a companion on the same device and challenge them to a classic international penalty format. Take alternating shots across five complete tournament rounds. If scores remain tied after five rounds, the engine automatically switches to a sudden death system.
  • The "Psych-Out" Feature: In 2 Player mode, whenever a player takes their high-stakes fifth penalty or is trying to stay alive in Sudden Death, the goalkeeper enters a psych-out routine. The keeper can change dynamic sprint vectors, fake left or right, or momentarily freeze to trick the kicker.

How to Play & Ball Controls

This web application interprets custom touch events and mouse clicks to map vector velocity onto the match ball:

  • Click or touch and hold directly on the football at its penalty spot.
  • Quickly swipe upwards towards the target framework.
  • To Curve the Ball: Swipe in an explicit arc structure. The distance between your swipe pathway and a direct straight path line is continuously measured to calculate ball rotation, bending the ball's flight path mid-air.
  • Be careful with your launch power—swiping with excessive speed or force can loft the ball clean over the crossbar!
